The sun burnt hot on my face. I called out to others. I heard them walk by.

But I couldn’t see them. Because I was born blind.

Even though I couldn’t see it, I felt their pity. But this is just the way my life is. Nothing will ever change… or will it?

Then I heard voices: “Who sinned? This man or his parents?” A different voice answered:

“Neither this man nor his parents sinned, but this happened so that the works of God might be displayed in him…I am the light of the world.”
